How to enter flash modes

  • Download/ODIN mode: Disconnect battery, then reconnect, plug into power and hold Power + Vol+ + Vol-. Might work without disconnecting battery, my unit is very weird.
  • Recovery mode: Shut down the device. Hold Power and Volume Up, then release once you feel a vibration

Notes

  • This device's SoC is supported by the linux-omap Mainline fork. No DT for the device seems to be available in sources, have to write one.
  • No FOSS driver available for PowerVR SGX530, so no 3D Acceleration
