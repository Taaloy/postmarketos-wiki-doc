Mainline Kernel Status

Status Hardware Info
Y Power Management Uses X-Powers AXP288 Controller , mainline driver exists.
Y Touch Screen Silead GSL1680. The Silead driver is in mainline, however doesn't work on this tablet so onitake's implementation is used instead.
Keyboard Dock Untested, however the dock should work fine as it worked on the Hi10 Pro.
Y USB Gadget/OTG Synopsys DesignWare Core SuperSpeed USB 3.0 Controller. Mainline kernel exists and works fine.
N SDHCI Unreliable, sometimes it does work, but most of the time are just errors.
P Wi-Fi Realtek RTL8723BS, driver is in staging, but buggy. Same problem as pine-dontbeevil
Y GPIO keys Volume up/down and Power button works fine.
Y Bluetooth Realtek RTL8723BS, mainline support existed in 4.19.
Y Sound bytcr-rt5651, mainline support existed in 4.16. Can also be replaced by Sound Open Firmware , however it is very buggy and doesn't seem to work properly.
Light Sensor Capella CM3218, there's currently no mainline driver available yet. There was a patch by the company but never made it to mainline .
N Sixaxis Bosch BMA250 , the driver is in mainline, however upon loads, the driver loaded and errored due to invalid chip detected by the driver.
Camera OV2680 and OV8858. There used to be drivers for both of these cameras in staging (AtomISP), however it has been removed from torvalds tree due to inefficiency and not working great (lambdadroid). As of v5.8 onwards the AtomISP v2 came back, but lacks of testers and developers...
Y GPU Intel Integrated Graphics, works as expected.
GPS Broadcom BCM47521. No driver is currently available in Torvalds tree.

How to enter flash mode

DNX Fastboot

  • Power on the system, wait until the Chuwi logo appears.
  • Press Vol Up + Down at the same time.

Android Fastboot

  • Power on the system
  • Wait until multiboot screen appears, then click to Android.
  • As soon you click "OK", hold down the Vol Down key until the display shows the Android mascot laying down open.

Installation

One of these options will remove your operating system, think twice.

Install to Android

  • Boot to Android fastboot
  • After that, proceed to flash using pmbootstrap .
