      Users owning this device

      Who made it?

      There are a file call APDB_MT6755_S01_alps-mp-m0.mp7_W16.19_ENUM in the stock of some variant of this phone.The file name indicate the phone is made by alps and it's name/codename is MT6755_S01_alps-mp-m0.mp7_W16.19.

      What is this phone

      This phone is a generic phone from china and it has been sold with many different names/variants.

      Identify the phone

      In the stock rom of the variants , the hostname/device is seted to pri6750_66t_m which is likely the name/codename of the phone.

      Unlock Bootloader

      WARNING: Make Sure you are running the stock rom/recovery before you unlock the bootloader otherwise it will not work.
      Only tested in ASK SP618's stock rom
      Dont work on Bluboo Maya Max's stock rom

      fastboot oem unlock
