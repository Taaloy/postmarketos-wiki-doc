How to enter flash mode

Power off the device, then press and hold Power + Volume Down together for a few seconds.

Installation

Follow the installation guide to build and install.

What's not working

  • Cellular
  • Camera
  • GPS
  • Sensors
  • IR
  • Sharp panel varient, but JDI panel varient works good
  • WiFi is unstable and the signal is poor (in some cases)
  • Built-in speakers and microphone, only headphone jack works

Current Progress

IR

The IR led can be lighted up with ir-ctl tool. Currently not test with real IR receivers.
