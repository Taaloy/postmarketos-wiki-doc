      From postmarketOS Wiki

      Samsung Exynos 3475 is a mobile SoC announced by Samsung in 2015. It is manufactured using a 28nm process technology. The chip powers several low-end devices.

      Notes

      Display

      There isn't any support for DECON and DSI yet, so panel drivers aren't a thing. However, display is known work with simple-framebuffer (simpledrm) with most devices.
