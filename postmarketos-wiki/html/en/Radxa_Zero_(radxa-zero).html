Installation

Follow https://wiki.radxa.com/Zero/dev/maskrom#Enable_maskrom to flash u-boot or tow-boot to mmcblk1boot0, then write pmos to sdcard or emmc. tow-boot: https://github.com/tvall43/Tow-Boot/releases/download/radxa-zero/Tow-Boot.noenv.bin
