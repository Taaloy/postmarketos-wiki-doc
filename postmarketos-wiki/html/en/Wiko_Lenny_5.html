How to enter Recovery mode

  1. Switch off your device.
  2. Press and keep pushed Volume Up and Power for 10 seconds, then release Power button.
  3. While keeping Volume Up pushed, press once Power to switch on the device.
  4. You should now enter recovery mode.

How to enter fastboot mode

  1. Use the above method to enter Recovery mode.
  2. Use Volume up/down to navigate and select "Reboot to bootloader", press Power to validate.
  3. The device should now reboot in Fastboot mode.
