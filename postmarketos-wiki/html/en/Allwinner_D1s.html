Allwinner F133 (also known as D1s) is a soc that smaller to D1-H. D1-H has hdmi but d1s dont.

Differences D1-H and D1s

D1s has built in 64 MB of DDR2 memory but D1-H dont.

Unlike D1-H , D1s is leak of Tensilica HiFi4 DSP , HDMI output and 1x I2S port.

D1-H use LFBGA package . D1s use eLQFP128 package.
