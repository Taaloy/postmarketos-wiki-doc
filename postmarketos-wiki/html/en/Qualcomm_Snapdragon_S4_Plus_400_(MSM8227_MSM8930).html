Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 Plus/400 (MSM8227/MSM8930)

From postmarketOS
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Other Qualcomm chips from the S4 series; similar to the Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 Plus (MSM8960) and Qualcomm Snapdragon 600 (APQ8064).

The MSM8225/MSM8625 (Snapdragon S4 Play) appears to be more similar to the Snapdragon S1 (MSM7627).
