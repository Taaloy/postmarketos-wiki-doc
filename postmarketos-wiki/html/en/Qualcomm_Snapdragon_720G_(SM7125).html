SM7125 (Snapdragon 720G) is a premium mid-range Qualcomm SoC released in 2020. Internal msm codename for this platform is atoll . The mainline kernel for SM7125 is based on and reuses drivers from Qualcomm Snapdragon 7c (SC7180) because of their similarities.
