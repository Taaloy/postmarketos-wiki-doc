      RK3188 is a Rockchip SoC released in 2013. There are two lower-performance variants of RK3188 named RK3128 and RK3126 released in 2014.

      Mainlining

      This basic support for the SoC family has been mainlined and further improvement is discussed on the linux-rockchip mailing list (archive, patchwork).
