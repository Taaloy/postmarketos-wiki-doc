Snapdragon 778G (SM7325), Snapdragon 778G+ (SM7325-AE) and Snapdragon 782G (SM7325-AF) are premium mid-range Qualcomm SoCs released in 2021 and 2022. It is part of the Kodiak family of Qualcomm chips and has the codename Yupik. SM7325 is software-wise very similar to the Qualcomm SC7280. Additionally, the Qualcomm QCM6490 also exists as an IoT variant of the chip, offering extended software support and sharing similarities with SC7280/SM7325 as well.
