The S905X/S905W/S905Z/S905L/S805X/S805Y are very similar to each other. The S905Z, used in the Amazon Fire TV 3 (amazon-needle), seems to be identical to the S905X except for the lack of ethernet,VP9 hardware decoding).There are also a slightly higher clock variant (CPU cores up to 2 GHz) available as S905W and a slightly lower clock variant (CPU cores up to 1.2 GHz) available as S805X.There are also a variant that lack VP9 decoding, camera interface and TS inputs and it is called S905L.The S805Y used in Xiaomi Mi TV Stick (xiaomi-aquaman), seems to be identical to the S805X.

There are also -h variant of S905X/S905W/S905Z/S905L/S805X/S805Y. it is a variant of the soc that has include Dolby/DTS licenses.
