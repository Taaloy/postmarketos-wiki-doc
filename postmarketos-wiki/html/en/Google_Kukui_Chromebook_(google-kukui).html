Installation

See Chrome OS devices page.

Audio

Overall in good condition, but it is missing jack detection support and sometimes headphones or/and headset mic. See device pages for more details.

Camera

It currently works only on laptops and convertible devices and doesn't work on tablets. This is because of camera being USB on laptops and CSI on tablets.
