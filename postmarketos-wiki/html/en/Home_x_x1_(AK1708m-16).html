How to enter flash mode

Installation

How to enter flash mode

0. unplug the power

1. Connect usb port that has labeled OTG to your pc via usb male a to usb male a.

WARNING: Make sure the 5v vcc pin aint connected in the cable otherwise it might will burn when u plug the cable to it.


2. Holding the button that has labeled update (There 2 button r labeled update so press either one) while u power it on and u r done.


Jtag

To get JTAG working, solder wires the jtag points that is labeled on the front side of the pcb.(see File:Front pcb of Home x x1.jpeg)

UART

The baud rate is 115200. To get UART working, solder wires the uart points that is labeled on the back side of the pcb.(see File:Back of Home x x1 pcb.jpeg)
