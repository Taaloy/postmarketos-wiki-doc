How to enter flash mode

Use ADB to reboot into bootloader or reboot to bootloader with TWRP using Vol + and Power buttons

Installation

Use pmbootstrap flasher flash_rootfs , then pmbootstrap flasher flash_kernel while in bootloader (fastboot) mode.

ADB sideloading seems to be broken, initramfs-extra doesn't sideload.)
