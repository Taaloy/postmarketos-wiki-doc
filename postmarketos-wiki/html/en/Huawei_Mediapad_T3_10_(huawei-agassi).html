      Users owning this device

      Bootloader unlocking

      WARNING: Backup ALL of your tablet flash! According the guide you will flash aboot/sbl and other important partitions via EDL. If you don't want to miss your IMEI/MAC or you want to roll back to android, backup all partitions

      1) Disconnect battery
      2) Short EDL points
      3) Connect battery
      4) Backup whole flash

        edl rl backup

      5) Download generic bootloader for your device from bootloaders repo
      6) Flash bootloader partitions to device

        edl wl AGS-LXX

      7) Reboot to fastboot (Power+Vol -)
      8) Start unlocking procedure

        fastboot oem unlock
      WARNING: Next step will wipe userdata!

      9) Continue unlocking procedure 

        fastboot oem unlock-go

      Your device is unlocked now. But it won't boot into android :D

      LK2nd

      Use this guide guide
      To build LK2nd for this device you need to add additional option to make 

      make TOOLCHAIN_PREFIX=... lk2nd-msm8952 LK2ND_ADTBS="msm8917-huawei-agassi.dtb" LK2ND_COMPATIBLE="huawei,agassi"
