How to enter flash mode

  • On your device follow below steps:
  • Open Settings >> System >> About Phone
  • Tap on the menu Build Number until you see a toast message “You are now a developer”.
  • Now you will see a new menu Developer Options and tap on it.
  • Look for a menu OEM unlocking and tap on it.
  • Connect your phone to the charger.
  • Now power off your device.
  • Press VOLUME UP and POWER button together.
  • When it vibrates release the POWER key alone and keep holding the VOLUME UP button.
  • If you see an android bot on your screen you are on fastboot mode and ready for flashing.
