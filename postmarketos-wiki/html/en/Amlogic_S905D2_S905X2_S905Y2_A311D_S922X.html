      The S905D2/S905X2/S905Y2/A311D/S922X SoCs are media player SoCs mainly used in Android set-top boxes.

      A311D/S922X are from G12B SoC family but the rest of them are part of G12A.However,they are very similar to each other .Here are the differences between them:


      Here is the difference between them :

      • S905D2: Enhanced audio (from AXG), 1x USB 3.0 or 1x PCIe 2.0, built-in 1G MAC and 100M PHY, two video decoders (4Kx2K@60fps + 1080P@60fps)
      • S905X2: Similar to S905D2 but without DVB interface and DSI
      • S905Y2: Similar to S905X2 but without built-in Ethernet
      • S922X: Similar to S905X2 but with 4 X Cortex-A73@1.8GHZ,2 X Cortex-A53@1.9GHZ (Kind of like Amlogic S912 in term of performance range)
      • A311D: Similar to S922X but with NPU

      There are also -h variant of S905D2/S905X2/S905Y2. it is a variant of the soc that has include Dolby/DTS licenses.
