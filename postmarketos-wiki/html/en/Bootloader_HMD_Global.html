Hikari Calyx UU4 Method

First request your bootloader unlock here and then follow this guide if you're on Linux.

MD5 Method

Get your device's serial number (e.g. adb get-serialno), get the md5 checksum of it and run fastboot oem key YOUR_MD5_SUM in fastboot mode. Then unlock with fastboot flashing unlock.
