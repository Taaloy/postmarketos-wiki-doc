How to enter system modes

  • For fastboot mode, press both volume keys and power key simultaneousely. After that, choose fastboot in the Pantech Menu.
  • For Recovery mode, press volume up key and power key together.

Installation

Recovery Zip installation is the most easiest. SD Card installation also works.


Problems

The device's custom android firmwares all use the stock kernel. However, the stock kernel only builds/boots well with *Only* with *arm-eabi-4.6*. With other versions, an error occurs while the build, or it simply does not work. Using the kernel built with arm-eabi-4.6 and the msm-fb-refresher package, it is possible to see the postmarketOS logo for a short time, and while lsusb and pinging from pc works, ssh does not connect for unknown reason. After that short time the device turns off and shows the VEGA (Manufacturer) logo, and more seconds pass, it shows the pmOS logo again, and the loop goes on.

Mainline?

The Soc seems to be on mainline kernel (msm8960), however because of unknown UART and no person to work with, this seems to be hard.
