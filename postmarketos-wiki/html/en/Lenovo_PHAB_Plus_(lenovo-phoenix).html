Variants

Lenovo PHAB Plus has two variants and the only difference is different software:

  • PB1-770M (Most common, available to purchase worldwide)
  • PB1-770N (Only sold in China)
  • PB1-770P (Only sold in China, the rarest)

The device was also sold in Japan as the Yamada Everypad III, the only difference is in the software.

Re-brands of the device was also sold in China, one of them is a Perfect one, the only difference is also in the software.
