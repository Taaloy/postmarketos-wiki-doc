Helio P35 (MT6765), Helio G35 (MT6765G),Helio P22 (MT6762), Helio A22 (MT6762M), Helio G25 (MT6762G) are low-end mobile MediaTek SoCs released in 2018 and 2020.

Helio P22T (MT8768T) is low-end tablet MediaTek SoC released in 2018.
