Alternative names

The Samsung Galaxy Core 4G is the same device (SM-G386F) as this one. It may only be a local variation for France.

How to enter flash mode

  • Press and hold Volume Down + Home + Power. Then press Volume Up to confirm.

Unfortunately, heimdall doesn't work properly with this device, so you have to use Odin on Windows to flash it.
