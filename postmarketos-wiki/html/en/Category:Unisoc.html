Unisoc (Shanghai Unisoc Technologies Co., Ltd.) is a Chinese fabless semiconductor company headquartered in Shanghai. It designs system-on-a-chip (SoC) products, primarily using the ARM architecture licensed from ARM Holdings for mobile, IoT, and AI applications.
