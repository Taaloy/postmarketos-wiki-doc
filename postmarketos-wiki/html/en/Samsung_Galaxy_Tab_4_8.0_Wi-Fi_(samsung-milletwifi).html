How to enter flash mode

Download mode: When turned off hold Power + Volume Down + Home

Recovery mode: When turned off hold Volume Up

Installation

Install Lk2nd/lk1st via Heimdall to get `lk2nd` working as your bootloader. This removes issues with device-specific quirks and makes booting this device more consistent with other devices.

Installation of pmOS from that point is being discussed in the Matrix chatroom and pmaports!4419.

Notes

Unclear on the hardware still, namely the display. I'm assuming things will be pretty identical to the 10.1". The display will be different, but the touchscreen controller works for up to 13" screens, so I'm assuming the atmel_mxt_1188s is also used in this device.
