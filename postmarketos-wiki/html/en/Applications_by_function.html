      Non-free

      Most of these do not have a client API, and do not align with postmarketOS principles. For the ones with a mobile site (see "Mobile site?" column), you may be able to use a web app browser like Tangram to use them similarily to a native app.

      App/service GNOME KDE/Kirigami Others Mobile site? Free alternatives
      Discord 🚩 This platform does not have a client API. N Matrix or Revolt
      Reddit 🚩 This platform does not have a client API. P Lemmy or Kbin
      TikTok 🚩 This platform does not have a client API. N N
      Tumblr 🚩 Y
      Twitter 🚩 This platform does not have a client API. Y Mastodon
      WhatsApp 🚩 This platform does not have a client API. Y

      Unofficial clients for apps with no official client API

      There exist unofficial clients for a few of the apps mentioned above (most notably Discord); however, they are usually against the ToS of these services, and may get your account banned. For this reason, they are not included in this table. Consider switching to a free alternative, or research at your own risk.
