How to enter flash mode

This device has ununlockable bootloader and requires exploiting the vunerability in bnr_recovery, also couldn't even boot to bootloader, so most likely flashing is impossible.

You can zero out laf, as on LG_G3_Europe_(lg-d855), but it will do nothing but removing the laf download mode.

Installation

Installation_from_recovery_mode

TWRP installation

Use PurpleDrake to get temproot (but permaroot doesn't work so ^C after that), then `adb push` twrp image and aboot into /temp and then dd them as in twrp installation instructions from adb shell)
