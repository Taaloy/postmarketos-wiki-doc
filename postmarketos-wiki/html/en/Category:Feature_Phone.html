Category:Feature Phone

Typical characteristics of feature phones include low-end SoCs, a (numeric) hardware keypad and lack of touchscreen hardware. Many feature phones don't have enough RAM to boot Linux, but those that come with Android or KaiOS preinstalled can do that and are a potential porting target for postmarketOS.
