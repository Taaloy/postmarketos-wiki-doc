Accessibility: 2020 Digital accessibility quick scan report

From postmarketOS
Jump to navigation Jump to search

As part of the funding received in 2020 by NLnet through the NGI0 PET Fund , postmarketOS has gotten a Digital accessibility quick scan report from the Accessibility Foundation. It was decided to review the installation process with the on-device installer , and the following reboot into osk-sdl and the Phosh user interface, including our custom welcome app . The report is based on said installation and first run experience, as shown in Martijn's demo video , starting at 2:30.

The report gives a good idea of what one needs to look out for with accessibility in mind. In particular, we need to work towards making our stack usable with a screenreader and look into improving contrast in the UIs.
