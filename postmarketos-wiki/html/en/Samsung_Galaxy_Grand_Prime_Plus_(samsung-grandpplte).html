How to enter flash mode

While turned off, hold Power+Home+Volume Down, then a menu will appear. Press Volume Up to proceed to flash mode.

UART

Accessing UART is possible through the microUSB port by putting a 619kÎ© resistor between the ID pin and ground. Read Serial debugging for more on that. Baud rate is 115200.
