General description

The 3rd generation OMAPs (3430/3530/3630), all use a single-core ARM Cortex-A8 CPU.
They have a PowerVR SGX530 integrated 3D graphics accelerator, and also have an Image, Video, Audio (IVA2) accelerator. Most of the family also feature a TMS320C64x+ DSP.
