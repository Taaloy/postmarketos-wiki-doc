About Gini

Gini is a brand of smartphones which is exclusively being soled by the company Pelephone Communications Ltd. in Israel.

The Gini brand is supposedly owned by the company Pelephone Communications Ltd., which is owned by Bezeq The Israeli Telecommunication Corp Ltd., which is owned by Searchlight Capital Partners.

Pelephone

Pelephone Communications Ltd. is a telecom service provider operating in Israel; it was founded in 1986 by the companies Motorola and Tadiran.

In 2004 Bezeq The Israeli Telecommunication Corp Ltd. acquired full ownership of the Pelephone company.

Bezeq

Bezeq The Israeli Telecommunication Corp Ltd. was founded in 1984 by the Israeli government and was fully owned by it.

In 2019 Searchlight Capital Partners acquired the Bezeq company.

Searchlight

Searchlight Capital Partners is a private equity firm based in Canada, United Kingdom and United States; it was founded in 2010.
