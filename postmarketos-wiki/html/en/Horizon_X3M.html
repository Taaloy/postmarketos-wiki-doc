The Sunrise 3.0 (X3M) is a highly-integrated, high-performance and power-efficient artificial intelligence System-on-Chip (SoC) powered by Horizon Robotics. Based on the dual-core BPU with Bernoulli-architecture, the X3M supports real-time pixel-level video segmentation, structured video analysis and attention-based vision perception. Video streams (max. resolution up to 8Mpixel@30fps) inputs from MIPI CSI-2 Receivers (Rx) or DVP are processed by the image signal processing (ISP) and video processing units before being passed to BPU for artificial intelligence inference computing such as video object detection, sentiment segmentation, scene parsing, etc.
