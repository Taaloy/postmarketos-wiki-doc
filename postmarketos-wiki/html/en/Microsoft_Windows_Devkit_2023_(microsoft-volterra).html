      How to boot into UEFI

      Press and hold the circle button that's recessed into the device. Then press the power button (pill form button that sticks out).

      Note that to see the output for UEFI, you NEED to use the Mini Displayport connection.

      While you are there, make sure UEFI Secureboot is disabled. pmOS will not be able to boot without it being disabled.

      How to boot from USB

      Press and hold the pill form button that's recessed into the device. Then press the power button (pill form button that sticks out).
