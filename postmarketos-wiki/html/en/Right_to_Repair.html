Introduction

If you legally own something, you can do whastever you are pleased, because it is yours.

The misleading concept of having a special "right", so called, to repair your own devices is idiotic, misleading and makes us to "want" to be dependent on a "parental" government as if we are silly irresponsible toddlers.

You are right

If a manufacturer provides a device that is difficult or not possible for most of us to repair, then it is our responsibility to buy from manufacturers that do provide modular and reparable devices (e.g. Category:PINE64 and Category:Purism).
