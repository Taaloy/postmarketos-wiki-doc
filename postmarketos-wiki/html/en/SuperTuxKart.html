SuperTuxKart is a kart racing game that runs fine on touch screens on devices with 3D acceleration with some tweaking.

SuperTuxKart

SuperTuxKart is available from Alpine's community repository and can as such be installed via: 

# apk add supertuxkart

Tweaks

Touch controls

By default you will likely not be presented with any touch controls once you enter a race. For touch controls to show up, you need to enable them manually in settings.
