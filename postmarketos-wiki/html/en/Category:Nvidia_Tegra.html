      GPU

      Tegra 2/3/4 use grate driver.

      Tegra K1 and newer use nouveau and it is broken. (See https://gitlab.freedesktop.org/mesa/mesa/-/merge_requests/2960)

      However, all Tegra SoCs share drm driver.
