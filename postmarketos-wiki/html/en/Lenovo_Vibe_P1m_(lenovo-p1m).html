How to enter flash mode

  • 1. Fully power off the phone
  • 2. Hold the Power button and the Volume Down button until a menu with three options appears
  • 3. Choose the second option (Fastboot Mode) with the Volume Down button and confirm with the Volume Up button
  • 4. Connect the phone over USB and proceed to flashing with fastboot


Installation

Alternative method of flashing (install TWRP first):

  • 1. Do steps 1-3 from the first instructions but choose the first option (Recovery mode)
  • 2. Tap "Advanced"
  • 3. Tap "ADB Sideload" and swipe to start
  • 4. Connect the phone via USB
  • 5. pmbootstrap install --android-recovery-zip
  • 6. pmbootstrap flasher --method=adb sideload
