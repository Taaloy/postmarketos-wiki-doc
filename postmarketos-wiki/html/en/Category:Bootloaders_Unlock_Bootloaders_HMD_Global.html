      Most HMD (Nokia) devices sadly can't be unlocked with any official methods anymore. This page lists unofficial methods for some devices.

      Model Unlock method
      Nokia 1 (nokia-frt) / 1 Plus MD5 Method
      Nokia 1.3 Just fastboot oem unlock without any key
      Nokia 2 / 2 V Hikari Calyx UU4
      Nokia 2.2 mtkclient
      Nokia 2.3 mtkclient
      Nokia 3 (nokia-ne1) MD5 Method
      HMD Global Nokia 3.1 (nokia-ES2) / 3.1 Plus Hikari Calyx UU4
      Nokia 3.2 Just fastboot oem unlock without any key
      Nokia 4.2 (nokia-panther) Just fastboot oem unlock without any key
      Nokia 5 (nokia-nd1) Hikari Calyx UU4

      Nokia 5.3 See this XDA thread
      HMD Global Nokia 6 (nokia-ple) Hikari Calyx UU4

      Hikari Calyx UU4 Method

      First request your bootloader unlock here and then follow this guide if you're on Linux.

      MD5 Method

      Get your device's serial number (e.g. adb get-serialno), get the md5 checksum of it and run fastboot oem key YOUR_MD5_SUM in fastboot mode. Then unlock with fastboot flashing unlock.
