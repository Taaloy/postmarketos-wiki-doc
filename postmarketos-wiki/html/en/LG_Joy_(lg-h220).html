Additional info

Entering "Factory data reset mode"

  1. Turn off the device
  2. Press and hold Volume Down + Power
  3. Release Power when LG logo appears
  4. While still pressing Volume Down, press and hold Power again
  5. Release Volume Down when Factory Data Reset menu appears

Entering "LG Download mode"

  1. Turn off the device
  2. Press and hold Volume Up
  3. Connect the USB cable
  4. Release Volume Up when Download mode appears
