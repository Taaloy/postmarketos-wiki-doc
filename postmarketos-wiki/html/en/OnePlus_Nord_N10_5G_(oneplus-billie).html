Users owning this device

How to enter flash mode

Hold Volume Down + Power for 5 seconds

Installation

  1. fastboot erase dtbo
  2. fastboot flash --disable-verity --disable-verification vbmeta vbmeta.img
  3. pmbootstrap init
  4. pmbootstrap install
  5. pmbootstrap flasher flash_kernel
  6. pmbootstrap flasher flash_rootfs --partition super
