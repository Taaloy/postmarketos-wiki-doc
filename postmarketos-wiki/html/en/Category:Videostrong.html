Videostrong is a STB manufacturer, mainly manufacturing TV boxes running either a modified version of AOSP or Android TV.

Some of the set-top boxes they sell are sometimes sold under the Videostrong brand, and sometimes they're sold under the Mecool brand. It also seems like some of their set-top boxes are sold under the ACEMAX brand (clone?).
