How to enter flash mode

Hold Power + Volume Down (while connected to the USB) to boot into the fastboot.

Installation

Mainline doesn't support mdsc, so boot only.

Mainline status

Mainline is very quirky and in very early state.

Booting

The booting works so far only through fastboot boot (don't know why) and only with an dummy dtbo.
