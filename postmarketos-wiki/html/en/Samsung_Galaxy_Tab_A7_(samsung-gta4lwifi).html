Users owning this device

How to enter flash mode

Odin mode: shutdown tablet, press volume up and volume down and power button, then plug your device into USB cord plugged into your computer, the odin screen will show.

Installation

Use pmbootstrap to build your own installation image. 

$ pmbootstrap init
$ pmbootstrap install --fde
