      The S812/S812-B/S812-S/S805/S802 SoCs are media player SoCs mainly used in Android set-top boxes. S802 is the only chip in the Meson8 Amlogic SoC family. S805 is the only chip in the Meson8b .The rest of them are part of Meson8m2.However,they are very similar to each other .Here are the differences between them:


      Here is the difference between them :

      • S802: 4x 2GHz Cortex-A9
      • S805: Similar to S802 but with Cortex-A5
      • S812: Similar to S802 but with H.265 UHD Decoder
      • S812-S: Similar to S802 but with Dolby digital hardware decoding
      • S812-B: Unknown


      There are also -h variant of S812/S812-B/S812-S/S805/S802. it is a variant of the soc that has include Dolby/DTS licenses.
