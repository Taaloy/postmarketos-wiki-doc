      Hacking the F(x)tec Pro1

      Both QX1000 and QX1050 are hacker-friendly devices as:

      • It comes unfused from the factory, meaning user can boot whatever they like without needing the binary to be signed by the vendor.
      • bkerler's fantastic EDL tool contains the firehose binary for the device, you can use this to flash individual partitions or completely switch up the partition table.
      • It works with EDL Deep Flash cable which is disabled on most retail devices.
