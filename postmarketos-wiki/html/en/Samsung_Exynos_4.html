Samsung Exynos 4

From postmarketOS
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Exynos 4 family of chips consists of the Exynos 4210 released in 2011 as well as the Exynos 4412 released in 2012, alongside with its dual-core variant - the Exynos 4212. The Exynos 4415 was released two years later in 2014.

The Exynos 4210, 4212 and 4412 are relatively well supported in mainline, and share a fair bit of code. Support for the Exynos 4212 has been re-added fairly recently (patchset), and has yet to make it to a stable kernel. Support for the Exynos 4415, however, was dropped as there were no devices in mainline that used it. In postmarketOS, the mainline kernel is packaged as linux-postmarketos-exynos4PMOS.
