How to enter flash mode

  • Turn the device completely off.
  • From a turned off device, press Power, volume down and home buttons at the same time. The blue interface of "download mode" will appear, press volume up to enter the mode.
  • connect the device to a PC via USB.

Installation

At this moment, installation of rootFs fails. When specifying the partition for rootFs (SYSTEM) heimdall fails the flash at 8%. This is usually fixed by configuring "sparse images" in APKBUILD, altough this doesn't seem to be doing anything. When flashing rootFs to RECOVERY partition flash fails at 94%.
